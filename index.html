About Us

Welcome to our website! At CNP-Predictions, we are a team of passionate individuals coming from Friedrichshafen, Germany, united by our shared love for football and technology. As the three founders, we have combined our expertise in artificial intelligence and neural technology to create a groundbreaking platform that revolutionizes the way predictions are made in the world of football. With our technology, we analyze insane amounts of data from the top 5 football leagues. Our state-of-the-art algorithms, powered by artificial intelligence, enable us to make accurate predictions and provide valuable insights for football enthusiasts and bettors alike. By leveraging the power of neural networks, we can identify patterns, trends, and key factors that influence match outcomes. We continually refine and enhance our algorithms to ensure the highest level of accuracy in our predictions.
